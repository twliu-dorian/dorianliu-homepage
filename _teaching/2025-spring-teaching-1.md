Teaching Assistant for Prof. Matthew Green's Spring 2025 course on practical cryptographic systems at Johns Hopkins. The course covers the design and implementation of real-world cryptographic protocols — block ciphers, authenticated encryption, key exchange, public-key infrastructure, secure messaging, and the common pitfalls that arise when deploying cryptography in practice.

Course materials and assignments: [matthewdgreen/practicalcrypto](https://github.com/matthewdgreen/practicalcrypto)
